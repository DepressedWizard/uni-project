The University of New Cairo

The University of New Cairo (UNC) is a leading institution in New Cairo, Egypt, known for its academic excellence and vibrant campus life. Offering a wide range of programs, UNC prepares students for global challenges through innovative teaching, research, and community engagement. Join us to be part of a dynamic academic community.

Course We Offer

We offer courses in Computer Science, Engineering, and Business.

Computer Science Program

The Computer Science program at the University of New Cairo equips students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the ever-evolving tech industry. Our curriculum covers core areas such as programming, algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and software development. With a strong emphasis on practical experience and research, students are prepared to tackle real-world challenges and innovate in various fields. Join us to explore the limitless possibilities of technology and drive the future of computing.s

Engineering Program

The Engineering program at the University of New Cairo offers a comprehensive education that blends theoretical knowledge with practical application. Our program covers various disciplines, including Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Chemical Engineering. Students engage in hands-on projects, cutting-edge research, and internships, preparing them to design and implement innovative solutions to global challenges. Join us to develop the skills and expertise needed to become a leader in the engineering field.

Business Program

The Business program at the University of New Cairo prepares students for success in the dynamic world of business. Our curriculum encompasses key areas such as management, finance, marketing, entrepreneurship, and international business. Through interactive coursework, case studies, and real-world experiences, students gain a deep understanding of business principles and practices. Join us to build a solid foundation for a successful career in the global marketplace and become a visionary business leader.
